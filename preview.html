About Us

Real estate development
rooted in quality

Dotta & Cunha is a residential development company based in Lisbon. We bring together architecture, engineering, and project management to create homes that stand the test of time.

Founded with a commitment to quality over quantity, we focus on carefully selected locations and thoughtful design — building spaces where people genuinely want to live.

Our team brings together decades of experience in construction, architecture, and real estate investment, ensuring every project is delivered to the highest standards.
